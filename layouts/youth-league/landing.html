The League

One Morning a Week. Real Basketball.

No tryouts. No travel. No five-day-a-week commitment. Just basketball, friends, and fun — one morning a week for eight weeks.

Three age groups so every kid plays with kids their size. Every kid gets equal playing time. Every kid gets a jersey. Every kid plays.

  • No mandatory fundraising. No concession duty. Just show up Saturday.
  • Position rotation every week — every player gets experience at guard, wing, and post.
  • Sportsmanship Value of the Week — we recognize character alongside competition.
  • Buddy requests — want your kid on a team with a friend? Just ask at registration.
