What's Included

What the Partnership Looks Like

Every partnership is tailored to the school's needs. Here's what most partners take advantage of:

After-School Skill Clinics

FCP coaches come directly to your school for structured, USA Basketball-aligned skill clinics. Dribbling, shooting, footwork, and game IQ — in 60–90 minute sessions.

Team & Group Discounts

School teams and groups of 5+ receive discounted rates on camps, leagues, and facility access. Ideal for school programs with limited budgets looking for professional-level training.

Player Development Tracking

Athletes enrolled through school partnerships get individual progress tracking. Coaches and parents receive updates on improvement milestones, helping schools demonstrate athletic development outcomes.

Film Study Sessions

Bring the mental side of basketball into your program. FCP coaches run game film review sessions that teach athletes to read defenses, make better decisions, and understand the full game.

Competitive League Access

Partner school athletes get priority registration for FCP's competitive youth and adult leagues with organized schedules, standings, and playoff brackets — all on a school-partner discount.

Character & Leadership

USA Basketball certified training goes beyond skills. Our coaches integrate character development, leadership, and competitive mindset training aligned with school community values.
