About FCP Sports

The Emerald Coast’s Premier Basketball Facility

FCP Sports was built with one mission: give every athlete on the Emerald Coast access to elite basketball development. Located in the heart of Fort Walton Beach, our facility features a full regulation court, professional coaching staff, and a proven curriculum that develops players at every age and skill level.

From 5-year-olds discovering the game for the first time to high schoolers chasing college scholarships, our coaches meet athletes exactly where they are — and take them further than they thought possible.

  • USA Basketball certified coaches with years of player development experience
  • 500+ athletes trained across Fort Walton Beach, Destin, Niceville, and beyond
  • AAU tournament access with full travel program and college coach exposure
  • School partnerships with Bruner Middle, Ruckel Middle, Pryor Middle, and Destin Middle
