FCP Sports is Fort Walton Beach's only dedicated basketball training facility — and it shows. When you walk in, you're not sharing space with a volleyball net or a gymnastics mat. This is a basketball-first environment, built for athletes who take the game seriously.

Located just minutes from the Okaloosa Island bridge and the FWB Landing, our facility sits at the geographic center of the Emerald Coast basketball community. Whether your athlete attends Choctawhatchee High, Fort Walton Beach High, or one of the area's elementary and middle schools, FCP Sports is the training home of choice for families throughout Okaloosa County.

## Fort Walton Beach's Basketball Hub

Fort Walton Beach has long served as the hub of Emerald Coast athletics — and FCP Sports reinforces that identity. Our full-court, climate-controlled facility features quality hardwood flooring, a scoreboard, professional-grade sound system, and a coaching staff that brings real-world competitive experience to every session.

Unlike the rec center programs scattered throughout the area, FCP Sports runs structured curricula. That means your athlete gets a skill assessment on day one, a personalized development plan, and measurable progress checkpoints built into every program cycle.

## Why Families in FWB Choose Us

Parents from Edwins Elementary, Pryor Middle, and Choctawhatchee High tell us the same thing: their athletes improve faster here because the environment demands it. Small group sizes mean more reps. Coaching attention means fewer bad habits go uncorrected. And because we're basketball-only, there's no off-season — your athlete can train year-round in a facility purpose-built for the sport.

Our proximity to Eglin AFB also means we work regularly with military families who need structured, consistent programming that fits around deployment schedules and the demands of base life. Fort Walton Beach is home, and FCP Sports is where its athletes train.

Every program at FCP Sports begins with an honest assessment.
