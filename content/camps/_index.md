## 2026-2027 Camp Schedule

FCP Sports runs basketball camps year-round at our dedicated, climate-controlled facility in Fort Walton Beach. Whether your child is picking up a basketball for the first time or chasing a college scholarship, we have a camp built for them.

Every camp below takes place at our indoor basketball facility — no shared gym time, no outdoor heat, no distractions. Just basketball.
