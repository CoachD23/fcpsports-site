## This Camp Exists Because Girls Deserve Better

Here is the reality of girls basketball in northwest Florida: most camps are co-ed programs where girls make up 20% of the roster and get 20% of the coaching attention. The drills are built for boys. The scrimmage teams are built around boys. Girls fill in the gaps.

FCP Sports built this camp to fix that. **This is the only girls-specific basketball camp in the entire Florida Panhandle.** Not a co-ed camp with a separate girls bracket. Not a "girls welcome" footnote on a boys camp flyer. This is five days of training designed from scratch for female basketball players.

Women's basketball is experiencing the biggest growth period in its history. The WNBA is expanding. NCAA women's tournament viewership broke records again this year. College scholarships for women's basketball are increasing. Your daughter deserves a camp that matches the moment.

## What Your Daughter Will Get That She Will Not Get Anywhere Else

**Equal reps, period.** Every drill rotation, every scrimmage minute, every coaching correction is built for the players in the building. There is no waiting behind a line of boys for your turn.

**Female-focused coaching approach.** Our coaching staff understands that the best female athletes respond to instruction that builds confidence alongside competition. We push hard and we build up — both at the same time.

**A peer group of serious female players.** Your daughter will train alongside other girls from across the Emerald Coast who take basketball seriously. That peer environment matters more than any single drill we run.

## Skill Development Areas

**Ball Handling:** Progressive dribbling combinations, pressure handling, off-hand development. We emphasize control at game speed — not flashy moves that fall apart under pressure.

**Shooting:** Form shooting, catch-and-shoot, off-the-dribble pull-ups, and free throws under fatigue. Video analysis on Friday so your daughter sees her mechanics and knows what to work on at home.

**Defense and Footwork:** Lateral slides, closeouts, help-side rotation, on-ball pressure. We spend more time here than most camps because footwork wins games at the high school level.

**Game Situations:** Scrimmages are coached. We stop play to teach reads, spacing, screen usage, and transition decisions. Every player learns to think, not just react.
