We do not run one giant camp and throw every kid together. FCP Sports runs four separate summer sessions, each built for a specific grade range:

Each session runs Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Drop-off begins at 8:45 AM. Pickup is at 3:00 PM sharp.

## What Makes This Different From a Rec Center Camp

Most summer basketball camps in northwest Florida happen in shared gyms — school cafeterias, church fellowship halls, recreation centers where your kid shares floor time with pickleball and yoga classes. FCP Sports is a **dedicated basketball facility** built for one sport. Climate-controlled, regulation hoops, hardwood court, proper scoreboard. When your child walks in, everything in the building exists for basketball.

We are located in Fort Walton Beach, 5 minutes from the Okaloosa Island bridge. Parents from **Destin, Niceville, Navarre, Crestview, and Pensacola** bring their kids here because there is nothing else like it in the area. Families stationed at **Eglin AFB and Hurlburt Field** know us well — we are the structured summer option that keeps kids developing when school is out.

## What Your Child Will Learn

**Ball Handling:** Stationary dribble work, on-the-move combinations, pressure dribble drills. By Friday, players execute crossovers, between-the-legs, and behind-the-back moves with confidence.

**Shooting Mechanics:** Form shooting from short range, spot shooting, off-the-dribble pull-ups. We use video on the final day so players can see their release and understand what to self-correct at home.

**Footwork and Defense:** Defensive slides, closeout technique, help-side positioning, pivots, jabs, drop steps. Footwork separates players at the next level, and most camps skip it.

**Basketball IQ:** Scrimmages are coached, not free-for-all. Players learn when to drive versus shoot, how to read a defense, how to set and use screens. We pause live play to teach, then resume.
