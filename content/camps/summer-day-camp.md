## Drop Off. Pick Up. Watch Them Glow.

Your kid spends the morning learning real basketball from experienced coaches. They come home tired, happy, and asking to go back tomorrow.

## Sessions & Dates

| Session | Dates | Grades | Hours | Price |
|---------|-------|--------|-------|-------|
| Session 1 | June 8–12 | Grades 3–5 | 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM | $129 |
| Session 2 | June 22–26 | Grades 6–8 | 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM | $149 |
| Session 3 | July 13–16 | Grades 6–10 | 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM | $149 |
| Session 4 | July 27–31 | Grades 3–8 | 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM | $129 |

**Younger kids (grades 3–5) do half days.** Three focused hours is the sweet spot — they learn, they play, they're done before lunch. No burnout, no babysitting filler.

**Older kids (grades 6+) go full day** with a lunch break, film review, and afternoon scrimmages.

Drop-off starts at 8:45 AM. Pickup is at noon (half-day) or 3:00 PM (full-day).

## What a Day Looks Like

**Morning (all sessions):**

- Warm-up and stretching
- Ball handling stations — dribbling, passing, control
- Shooting clinic — form, footwork, game-speed reps
- Water break
- Defensive fundamentals — slides, closeouts, positioning

**Afternoon (full-day sessions only):**

- Lunch break (bring your own)
- Film review or chalk talk
- 5-on-5 coached scrimmages
- Skills competition and cool-down

Monday starts with a skills assessment. Friday ends with a repeat — so your kid (and you) can see exactly what improved.
