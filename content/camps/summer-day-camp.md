## Daily Schedule

**Morning:** Warm-up, ball handling stations, shooting clinic, defensive fundamentals

**Midday:** Lunch break, film review

**Afternoon:** Coached scrimmages, skills competition

Skills assessment on Monday. Re-assessed Friday so your camper sees what improved.

## Discounts

- **Military / DoD families:** $20 off (Eglin + Hurlburt)
- **Sibling:** $20 off per additional child

Discounts stack.
