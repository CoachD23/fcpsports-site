## Basketball PE That Actually Counts

Florida homeschool families have to document PE — but most options are either gym memberships the parent supervises, or generic "drop-in" programs with no structure. **FCP Homeschool PE is different.** It's structured basketball curriculum, delivered by paid coaches, with a certificate of completion you can hand to your umbrella school.

### What Your Kid Gets

- **Two sessions a week** (Tue + Thu mornings) — enough to build real skill, spaced so it's not a grind
- **Small-group coaching** — never more than 10 kids per coach
- **Age-scaled curriculum** — K–2 learn motor skills and game basics; 3–5 build fundamentals; 6–8 work on game IQ and competitive skills
- **Written progress reports** — three per semester so you know exactly what your athlete is learning
- **Certificate of completion** — semester documentation of hours, skills covered, and attendance for your records

### Schedule

**Tuesdays and Thursdays, 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM**
September through May (school-year program, breaks for holidays).

Summer months: transition into our Summer Camps and Saturday League if you want your athlete to keep playing.
