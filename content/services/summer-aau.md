## NCAA Live Period Windows: Get Seen by College Coaches

The NCAA Live Period is the single most important opportunity in the high school recruiting process. During these windows — specific dates set by the NCAA — college coaches from every division can attend tournaments and evaluate prospects they cannot otherwise observe live.

FCP Sports **specifically selects tournaments during Live Period windows** where the concentration of college coaches is highest. Division I, II, and III programs send coaches to these events. If your athlete has college-level ability and is not getting seen during Live Period events, they are not being recruited. FCP Sports makes sure our players are in the room.
