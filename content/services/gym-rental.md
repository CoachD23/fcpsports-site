Book the gym and it's yours. No sharing with other groups. No scheduling around anyone else.

## What's Included

- Full regulation court (94' x 50')
- Scoreboard and sound system (Bluetooth)
- Game balls
- Locker rooms
- Climate control
- Free parking

## Time Blocks

- **1 hour** — quick practice or individual work
- **2 hours** — team practices, pickup games (most popular)
- **Half day (4 hours)** — tryouts, clinics, birthday parties
- **Full day (8 hours)** — tournaments, large events

Weekday rates are lower. Recurring weekly bookings get a discount.
