## Rent the FCP Sports Court for Any Event

The only rentable private basketball court in Okaloosa County, FCP Sports offers full facility access for teams, families, businesses, and individuals who need a premium basketball space on their own terms. No sharing the court with other groups. No scheduling around a school's aftercare program. You book the block, you own the gym.

Our regulation full-court gymnasium is available for rental any day of the week, subject to availability. Whether you need 90 minutes for a team practice, a half-day for tryouts, or a full Saturday for a birthday party that your kid will talk about for years — we can accommodate it.

## What's Included in Your Gym Rental

Every rental at FCP Sports includes:

- **Full regulation court access** — the entire 94' x 50' court, both baskets, full markings
- **Scoreboard** — functional electronic scoreboard for your event
- **Sound system** — Bluetooth-compatible audio for music or announcements
- **Court-approved basketballs** — set included with every rental
- **Locker room access** — separate men's and women's facilities
- **Parking** — ample free parking adjacent to the facility
- **Climate control** — fully air-conditioned; Fort Walton Beach summer heat is not your problem

Additional options: portable shot clocks, agility equipment, coach supervision, video recording setup.

## Rental Rates & Time Blocks

We offer four standard rental blocks:

- **1-Hour Block** — ideal for a quick practice or individual session
- **2-Hour Block** — the most popular option for team practices and pickup games
- **Half-Day Block (4 hours)** — tryouts, clinics, birthday parties, small tournaments
- **Full-Day Block (8 hours)** — large events, multi-team tournaments, film shoots

Weekday rates are lower than weekend rates. Recurring weekly renters receive a discounted rate. Contact us for a quote based on your specific dates and needs.
