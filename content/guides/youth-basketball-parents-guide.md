## Section 1: Choosing the Right Program

Not all youth basketball programs are created equal, and the wrong fit can do more harm than good. This section helps you evaluate any program — ours or anyone else's — using a clear set of criteria:

- **Coach credentials and background checks** — the non-negotiable first question
- **Athlete-to-coach ratio** — anything over 10:1 in a youth setting is a red flag
- **Curriculum vs. supervised chaos** — how to tell the difference from a one-hour visit
- **Age-appropriate structure** — what a 6-year-old needs versus what a 12-year-old needs
- **The progression pathway** — does this program have somewhere for your child to go next?

We also cover the specific programs available at FCP Sports: Little Ballers (K–2nd), Junior Program (3rd–5th), Elite Youth (6th–8th), and High School & Recruiting. You'll understand exactly which program fits your child's age and goals — and why.
