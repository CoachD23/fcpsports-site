## Two Programs by Grade

### Little Ballers — K through 2nd Grade

- 45-minute sessions
- Coordination and movement games
- Ball-handling basics: bouncing, catching, passing
- Shooting form fundamentals
- No scorekeeping, fun-first

### Junior Program — 3rd through 5th Grade

- Dribbling combinations and ball security
- Passing: chest, bounce, overhead
- Shooting off the dribble and from set positions
- Basic offensive and defensive concepts
- 3-on-3 and 5-on-5 game play

## What to Expect

- **Week 1:** Informal skills check-in so coaches can group by ability
- **Weeks 2–4:** Fundamentals and routines get established
- **Ongoing:** Progress updates from coaches at pickup

Sessions run after school and on weekends.
