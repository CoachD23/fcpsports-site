## Elite Youth Program — 6th through 8th Grade

### Skills Covered

- **Ball handling:** Multi-move combinations, speed dribbling, crossovers under pressure
- **Shooting:** Catch-and-shoot, pull-up off the dribble, free throw consistency
- **Defense:** On-ball pressure, help-side positioning, boxing out
- **Basketball IQ:** Reading screens, ball movement, offensive/defensive schemes
- **Film review:** Athletes watch their own game footage to fix habits

### Tryout Prep

We run tryout-simulation sessions before high school tryout season — the same drills and evaluation scenarios used at Fort Walton Beach High, Choctawhatchee, Niceville, Navarre, and Crestview.
