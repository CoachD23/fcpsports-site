## Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

FCP Sports ("we," "us," or "our") operates fcpsports.org. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our site or register for programs.

### Information We Collect

- **Contact information** you provide (name, email, phone) when registering or contacting us
- **Usage data** collected automatically (pages visited, browser type, device) via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager
- **Email addresses** submitted through signup forms on our site

### How We Use Your Information

- To respond to inquiries and process program registrations
- To send program updates, schedules, and promotional offers (you can unsubscribe at any time)
- To improve our website and marketing based on usage data

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

- **GoHighLevel (GHL)** — contact management and email/SMS communications
- **Google Analytics (GA4)** — website traffic analysis
- **Google Tag Manager** — tracking and analytics tag management
- **Netlify** — website hosting and form processing

### Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send. To request deletion of your data, contact us at info@fcpsports.org.
