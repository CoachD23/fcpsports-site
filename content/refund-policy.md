We run a tight, professionally managed program. This policy exists so every family knows exactly where they stand before they register — no surprises, no exceptions after the fact. Read it once, register with confidence.

---

## Basketball Camps

| Cancellation Timing | Refund |
|---|---|
| 15+ days before camp starts | Full refund |
| 8–14 days before camp starts | 50% refund **or** full credit toward another FCP Sports camp |
| 7 days or less before camp starts | No refund, no credit |
| After camp has started | No refund for missed days, regardless of reason |

To cancel, email **info@fcpsports.org** or call **850.961.2323**. Cancellations are processed in the order received; your cancellation date is the date FCP Sports confirms receipt.

---

## Saturday League

| Cancellation Timing | Refund |
|---|---|
| Before the season starts | Full refund minus $15 administrative fee |
| Within the first 2 weeks of the season | 50% refund |
| After week 2 of the season | No refund |

League spots are limited. Late cancellations prevent other players from joining, which is why the window is firm.

---

## Private Lessons

- **24+ hours notice:** Reschedule at no charge. One free reschedule per booking window.
- **Less than 24 hours notice:** Lesson is forfeited. Full charge applies.
- **No-show:** Lesson is forfeited. Full charge applies. No exceptions.

If your coach cancels, you receive a full reschedule or refund — your choice.

---

## Gym Rental

| Cancellation Timing | Refund |
|---|---|
| 72+ hours before rental | Full refund minus $50 administrative fee |
| 24–72 hours before rental | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours or day-of | No refund |

Gym rental cancellations must be submitted in writing to **info@fcpsports.org**.

## If FCP Sports Cancels

If we cancel a program for any reason:

- **Full refund** within 5 business days — no questions asked.
- **Or** full credit toward any future FCP Sports program (your choice).

We will notify you by phone and email as soon as a cancellation decision is made.

---

## What Is Never Refundable

The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

- **T-shirts, uniforms, and equipment** once issued
- **Multi-week bundle savings** if you drop below the qualifying number of weeks (e.g., bundle pricing applied at 2 weeks; if you cancel one, the remaining week reverts to single-week price and the difference is not refunded)
- **Late fees or returned payment fees**
- **Credit card processing fees** ($5 per transaction)

---

## Weather & Illness

**Weather cancellations:** FCP Sports makes all weather-related calls. We will attempt to reschedule. If a reschedule is not possible, a **program credit** is issued — no cash refund. Credits are valid for 12 months.

**Minor injury or illness mid-camp:** No partial refund. A pro-rated credit may be issued at FCP Sports' sole discretion.

**Documented major medical emergency:** Full credit toward any FCP Sports program, valid for 12 months from the original program date. Written documentation from a licensed physician is required.

---

## How to Request a Refund

1. Email **info@fcpsports.org** with your name, your child's name, the program, and your reason.
2. We will confirm receipt within 1 business day.
3. Approved refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.
