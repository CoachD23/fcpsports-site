## NBA Veteran Kenny Anderson to Lead Monthly Basketball Clinics in Fort Walton Beach

*Former first-round draft pick brings elite-level training to Emerald Coast youth athletes beginning October 2026*

---

**FORT WALTON BEACH, FL — April 2026** — FCP Sports, the Emerald Coast's dedicated basketball training facility, today announced a series of monthly basketball clinics led by NBA veteran Kenny Anderson. The five-session clinic series runs October 2026 through February 2027 and is open to athletes in grades 6 through 12.

Anderson, a former first-round NBA draft pick, will lead each two-hour Saturday morning session at FCP Sports' 14,000-square-foot facility in Fort Walton Beach. The clinics focus on ball handling, decision-making, and the mental approach to competitive basketball — skills Anderson developed over a professional career spanning more than a decade.

"Kenny brings something our athletes can't get anywhere else on the Emerald Coast — real NBA experience applied directly to youth development," said Coach D, Head Coach and Founder of FCP Sports. "These aren't autograph sessions. These are working clinics where kids train alongside a player who competed at the highest level."

The clinics are scheduled for October 17, November 14, December 12, January 16, and February 13, from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Individual sessions are $49, or athletes can register for all five sessions at $199 — a savings of $46. A $20 military discount is available for families stationed at Eglin Air Force Base and Hurlburt Field.

FCP Sports is the only dedicated basketball-only facility in the Fort Walton Beach–Destin–Niceville corridor, serving youth athletes ages 5 through 18 with camps, leagues, training programs, and open gym sessions year-round.
